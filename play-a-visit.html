Getting to know each other

We know meeting someone for the first time can be intimidating, and going to a new church for the first time can be nerve-racking. We want to help make your first experience at Life Abundant a great one!

Service Times

Life Abundant meets every weekend on Sundays at 384 Concession 7 Rd. in Niagara-on-the-Lake:

  • Sunday School: Sundays at 9:00am
  • Church Prayer: Sundays at 10:30am
  • Worship Service: Sundays at 11:00am

What can I expect?

How long is a Life Abundant service?

In total, a Life Abundant service is about 60 minutes in length. Services begin with the Life Abundant band leading the church in music - song lyrics are projected onto the screens so you can sing along and/or engage with worship however you feel most comfortable. After the music portion of service is complete, one of our pastors will come out to share an encouraging and hope-filled message about Jesus.

What's the culture like at Life Abundant?

Sunday's at Life Abundant are exciting, casual, and relaxed. Come as you are and expect to feel welcomed as our guest.

What about my kids?

We believe that kids should have a blast at church every single week - and at Life Abundant, we make this a priority. The other thing we make a priority is your children’s safety. Because of that, we have a detailed check-in process for our Life Abundant program the first time that you visit. You’ll want to leave yourself an extra ten minutes to get signed in for the Life Abundant Kids experience. Kids ministry is offered at every Life Abundant service for kids ages infant through Grade 8.
